Todo All The Things

Todo All the Things is a simple, elegant, todo list.

It's free, opensource, self hostable, private, and minimal.

No Login ?

All your tasks are stored in your own browser, they're not visible to anyone else. You can configure your own storage like dropbox or nextcloud to sync lists between your devices.

Tag Syntax

You can enter tasks as a simple sentence.

reconfigure flux capacitor

But there's a special syntax to add fancy tags and formats to your tasks.

(A) task - description @project +context tag:value
