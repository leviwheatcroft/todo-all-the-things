Todo All The Things

Todo All the Things is an elegant self hosted todo list.

It's free, opensource, self hostable, private, and minimal.

No Login ?

All your tasks are stored in your own browser, they're not visible to anyone else. You can configure your own storage like dropbox or nextcloud to sync tasks between your devices.

Syntax

(A) task - desription @project +context !due tag:value

You can simply enter tasks as a simple sentence, but this syntax for due dates, projects, and contexts provides a much more readable list. All segments are optional.
